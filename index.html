SWAMI VIVEKANANDA

"AWAKE, ARISE, AND STOP NOT TILL THE GOAL IS REACHED"

Here's a Time line of Swami Vivekananda's Life:-

Name:- NARENDRANATH DATTA

Father:- Biswanath Datta, a renowned Attorney at The Calcutta High Court.

Mother-Bhubaneswari Devi, a devout housewife.

Residence:- 3, Gour Mohan Mukherjee Street, Calcutta,(India)

Birth:- 12th January, 1863, at Calcutta

Identification in short:-

A voracious reader, excelled in speed reading.

A person with prodigious memory (Shrutidhar),

An Indian Hindu Monk

The chief disciple of the 19th century Indian mystic Ramkrishna Paramhamsha.

A key figure to propagate Indian Philosophy & Yoga to the Western World.

The founder of the Ramkrishna Mission.

The author of many books, such as, Raja Yoga, Karma Yoga, Jnana Yoga, Bhakti Yoga, My Master, Lectures from Colombo to Almora

The composer of the prayer song-KHANDANA BHAVA-BANDHANA sung in the Mission at 6 p.m.

Education & Works-

1863-1879 -Schooling life, passed the College Entrance in 1881, studied the Vedas, the Upanishads, the Bhagawat Gita, the Ramayana, the Mahabharata and the Puranas, and developed a spiritual thirst.

In 1884-got a Bachelor of Arts Degree. Got acquainted with the Western Logic, Philosophy and European History. Studied the works of David Hume, Immanual Kant, Herbert Spencer, W.F.Hegel, John Stuart Mill, Charles Darwin and many more with Sanskrit scriptures and Bengali Literature.

During his education, he was influenced by Keshob Chandra Sen's Naba Bidhan Organization, a breakaway fraction of Brahma Samaj. In this cultic milieu, Narendranath became acquainted with western esotericism and developed a belief in formless God and a deprecation of idoltry, and rejected the Advaita Vedanta of identity with the absolute.

He met Ramkrishna(RK) in 1881 with disbeliefs on him and his intellectual quest of God became immense. Later with many an altercation, he grew ready to renounce everything for the sake of realising God and accepted Ramkrishna as Guru in 1884. In 1885 he took Ochre robes(Sannyas) from RK and was taught that service to men is the effective work to God. After the demise of RK in 1886 he was busy in organising the other disciples through austerity for becoming true followers of RK.

1888-1893 -took a life of Parivrajak , the Hindu religious life of a wandering monk, to have the frist-hand experience of Indian subcontinent's geography, economics, social structures, different religions etc. He developed sympathy for suffering and poverty.

1893-1897 -Journey to the West via Japan, China, Canada en route to the United States.

On 11th Sept, 1893, he delivered his great speech on Hinduism addressing the audience present as " the Sisters and Brothers" in the Parliament of Religions at Chicago. The theme of his lecture was universality and religious tolerance

1897-1899 -Came back India and founded RamKrishna Mission

1899-1902 -Again visited Europe and America with Sister Nivedita, his Irish disciple , delivered lectures and speeches for raising fund for RK MISSION and opened branches named Vedanta Societies in San Francisco and Shanti Ashram at California. In the mean time, his health broke down and came back to Belur RK Mission.

Death - He died on the 4th July,1902.

His Philosophy - Each soul is potentially divine. The goal is to manifest this divinity within by controlling nature, external or internal. Do this either by work, or worship , or mental discipline, or philosophy---- by one, or more, or all of these---and be free. This is the whole religion. Doctrines, or dogmas, or rituals, or books, or temples, or forms are but of secondary.

His birthday (12th Jan ) is celebrated as National Youth Day throughout the country.
